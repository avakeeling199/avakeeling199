### Hi, I'm Ava 👋

DPhil researcher at the University of Oxford, affiliated with the **Big Data Institute** and the 
**Leverhulme Centre for Demographic Science**.

---

**What I'm working on**

- 🏥 **NHS agent-based simulation** — building a large-scale ABM using Oxford data and LLMs to 
  model the NHS from patient and clinician level up to policy level, with a focus on health 
  disparities
- 🎲 **Aleatoric luck in the life course** — decomposing prediction error via learning curves 
  and power-law fitting to empirically bound the role of luck in life outcomes
- 🦠 **Epi-LLM** — integrating LLMs with agent-based epidemic modelling and behavioural game data

**Interests**

- Agent-based modelling & simulation
- Health inequalities and policy
- LLMs in population health contexts
- Computational demography
